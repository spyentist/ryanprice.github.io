Target description

This weather network will be available to those people who are in Eastern Idaho and are looking for up to date information about their surroundings and the forecast for their future.

Two Personas

Here is the description of Steve and Alex. Steve is a middle aged man who works about 3 cities away from where he lives, even though it is a 20 minute commute he likes to know which way will be able to give him a faster route to work. Alex is a young woman who loves going sight seeing and has had a recent interest in Eastern Idaho as it is not far from her hometown on the west side of Idaho. She will be looking at the small towns that give the most homely feel and have the greatest hospitality.

5 Senarios

  1. Steve checks the Price Networks Weather forecast every mornning to find out which route will be the best for him.
  2. Alex plans to go to the Gyser in Soda Springs and checks to find a weekend that is clear.
  3. Nichole tries to surprise Ryan with a weekend getaway to Lava Hot Springs and checks to find a weekend that is chilly but not snowing.
  4. Sam is looking for when the weather will finally clear up so he can start biking to school.
  5. Susie looks at the weather in Preston to see what it will look like for her family reunion.
