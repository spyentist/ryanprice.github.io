Site Purpose

This site will allow for people who travel to temples to find hotels that are very reasonable and are within a close proximity to the temple.
Those who visit the site are mainly going to be members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website is members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. They are looking to visit temples and have lodging close by. This website will have the information for these people to get reservations and have information about the temples themselves.

2 Personas

Steve - Steve is a temple worker who lives in Southern Colorado but works in the Albuquerque. He and his wife travel to Albuquerque one week out of the month to work in the temple. They have been looking for hotels that have the lowest price. After finding the Temple Inn and Suites website they have been able to stay in a consistent location that is close to the temple.

Alex - Alex is a young returned missionary who is looking to go on a temple hopping trip where she visits every temple on her way from her hometown to her college destination.
